Project Analysis | Get Gaming Orlando

Site Intent

The intent of the Get Gaming Orlando redesign to to make the website look more professional, fix any errors, increase traffic, and improve customer satisfaction.

The redesign process will start with the content; figure out what needs to stay and what could/should be removed. Next, I will focus on the layout of the site. By making the layout easier to navigate as well as look it, will help increase the site's usability. And then finally I will fix the site's aesthics by making it more cleaner as well as making it look more professional looking.
