Project Analysis | Get Gaming Orlando

Site Intent

The intent of the Get Gaming Orlando redesign to to make the website look more professional, fix any errors, increase traffic, and improve customer satisfaction.

The redesign process will start with the content; figure out what needs to stay and what could/should be removed. Next, I will focus on the layout of the site. By making the layout easier to navigate as well as look out, will help increase the User Usability. And then finally I will fix the site's aesthics by making it more cleaner as well as making it look more professional looking.

Targeted Audience

General main targeted audience is single male students between the ages of 13 to 25.

Persona One: Bob

  • Age: 18
  • Gender: Male
  • Occupation: Student
  • Education: High School
  • Tech Knowledge: High

User-workflow:

Bob's Playstation 3 just broke right before a new game is just about to come out. He wants to try to get his PS3 repaired before the new game is released so he opens Google Chrome and searches for a repair shop in Orlando. He then clicks on the site, views what they repair, and then finds the contact information and location.

Persona Two: Kevin

  • Age: 23
  • Gender: Male
  • Occupation: Student
  • Occupation: College
  • Tech Knowledge: High

User-workflow:

Kevin's Xbox 360 just overheated after a long game of Call of Duty. Frustrated he opens Mozilla Firefox and searches for a repair shop in Orlando. He then clicks on the site, views what they repair, and then finds the contact information and location.
